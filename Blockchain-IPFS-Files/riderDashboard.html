Help Center

How to request a ride?

Click on the "Request Ride" button in the top right corner of your dashboard. Enter your pickup and destination locations, then confirm your ride request.

How payments work?

All payments are processed securely through your wallet. You can add funds to your wallet or link a payment method for automatic top-ups when your balance is low.

Safety features

We prioritize your safety with features like real-time tracking, emergency contacts, and ride sharing with trusted contacts.

Rider ratings
